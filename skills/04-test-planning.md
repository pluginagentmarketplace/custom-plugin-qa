## Planning Process

1. **Understand Requirements** - Features to test
2. **Define Scope** - In/out of scope
3. **Assess Risks** - Critical areas
4. **Estimate Effort** - Time and resources
5. **Schedule** - Timeline planning
6. **Set Success Criteria** - Quality gates
