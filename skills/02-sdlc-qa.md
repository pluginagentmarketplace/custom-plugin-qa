# Software Development Lifecycle & QA

## SDLC Models

### Waterfall
- Sequential phases
- Distinct testing phase
- Documentation heavy
- Predictable timeline

### Agile
- Iterative development
- Continuous testing
- Sprint cycles
- Quick feedback

### DevOps
- Continuous everything
- Automated testing
- Fast feedback
- Quality throughout

## QA Role

✅ Requirements review
✅ Test planning
✅ Test execution
✅ Defect management
✅ Quality gates
✅ Continuous monitoring

## Key Principles

- Early involvement
- Continuous quality
- Risk-based approach
- Team collaboration
